## Read

This visit is about learning to read water in a place.

We are looking for the small evidence: where rain leaves too fast, where soil is
exposed, where a hollow wants to hold moisture, where a track or ditch is
accidentally moving water in the wrong direction.

Checkdam's work is about practical water retention: slowing, spreading, sinking,
and storing water in ways that suit the land. That can include check dams,
infiltration features, earth-integrated water bodies, and designs that change as
the site teaches us more.

This video is not final design advice. It is a field note: part observation,
part explanation, part invitation.

The bigger vision is simple. If AI gives us leverage, some of that leverage
should go into visible repair: water in the land, life in the soil, and evidence
we are willing to show.

Checkdam. Restore the water. Restore the land.
