# Product Truth: Living Water Skills

Date: 2026-06-02

## Elevator Pitch

Living Water Skills is a practical outdoor learning and logbook system for water-cycle restoration: learn small safe skills, capture field evidence, get the right level of review, and grow from noticing water to stewarding real places.

## Quick Start Guide

1. Choose a learner path: youth stage or adult level.
2. Create or select a place you can safely observe.
3. Capture a water observation with photo, voice, text, location, weather, and uncertainty.
4. Compare the evidence against the next checklist item.
5. Get AI feedback for early learning, then peer, guide, coach, or expert review for harder steps.
6. Turn reviewed observations into private or public story cards.

## One Sentence

Living Water Skills turns water-restoration learning into a safe, reviewed, evidence-backed outdoor logbook.

## User And Job

- User: people who want to help water-cycle restoration but need a safe, staged path from observation to action.
- Job: know what to learn next, capture evidence outside, get proportionate feedback, and avoid overclaiming competence.

## Truth Levels

### Proven

- Project notes exist under `docs/project-notes/water-restoration-skills-scheme/`.
- The app-plan clearly splits Living Water Handbook from Living Water Logbook.
- The review ladder is explicit: AI coach, peer/community member, Guide or Steward Coach, Design Reviewer / qualified expert.
- The plan includes youth stages, adult levels, advanced modules, practitioner roles, and evidence capture.
- The guardrails reject fake qualifications, unsupervised risky works, and automatic sign-off.

### Working But Unproven

- Static/clickable prototypes exist as private workbench direction, but this is not yet a public product.
- The first audience is still assumed to be local volunteers plus youth/family groups.
- Real accreditation, insurance, safeguarding, and legal structure are not complete.

### Aspirational

- A learner can ask "How do I get the next level at home?" and get a safe answer based on stage, location, evidence, permission, and risk.
- Field observations become reviewed public story cards that help water-restoration work spread.
- Community members with higher levels can review early evidence while specialists handle risk.

### False Or Stale

- This is not the RYA and should not imply RYA endorsement.
- This is not a shortcut to hydrology, ecology, engineering, safeguarding, or legal permission.
- AI cannot grant competence, machinery permission, or public truth.

### Private Only

- Raw locations, youth data, landowner details, and unreviewed field observations are private by default.

## Smallest Honest Pitch

Living Water Skills gives people a staged way to learn water-cycle restoration outside: observe, record, reflect, get safe feedback, and build evidence before claiming competence or publishing a story.

## Docs/Copy To Change

1. Product copy should lead with learning and logbook, not certification.
2. Public copy must make review and permission boundaries visible.
3. Advert scripts should use "learn, observe, capture, review, story" as the spine.
