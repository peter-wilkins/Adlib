## Read

The problem with AI chat is not that it cannot help you think. It can.

The problem is what happens next week, when you know you had the thought, but
you cannot find the thread anymore.

Continuum is a product direction built around continuity of thought. Instead of
forcing you to manage folders, dashboards, labels, and half-remembered chat
titles, the idea is to preserve the source history and help you re-enter the
right mental context quickly.

Every capture becomes part of an append-only event stream. The AI can summarise,
connect, annotate, and surface context, but the source remains recoverable.

That is the emotional promise: less reconstruction, less continuity anxiety,
more momentum restored.

Chat helps you think. Continuum helps you continue thinking.

Continuum. Preserve the thread.
