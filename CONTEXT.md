# AdLib

AdLib is the local audio advert workshop for turning product truth and approved
scripts into reusable audio assets, rendered adverts, and searchable evidence.

## Language

**Audio Asset**:
A small reusable clip, such as a spoken line, sting, sound effect, music bed, or
ambience layer, intended to be combined with other assets during editing.
_Avoid_: advert, render, final clip

**Rendered Advert**:
A complete edited advert made from one or more audio assets, with timing, mix,
background sound, effects, and pacing decisions already applied.
_Avoid_: asset, line, raw generation

**Asset Quality Gate**:
The review boundary that decides whether an audio asset or rendered advert is
usable enough for its next review step.
_Avoid_: feedback loop, AI review, preflight

**Audio Asset Quality Gate**:
The Asset Quality Gate for a small reusable clip before it is promoted for
editing into rendered adverts.
_Avoid_: advert review, campaign test

**Voice Audition**:
An Audio Asset Quality Gate mode that compares candidate voices or tone settings
using short approved lines before spending more generation effort on that voice.
_Avoid_: rendered advert test, paid audience test

**Rendered Advert Quality Gate**:
The Asset Quality Gate for a complete edited advert before it is promoted to
outside or paid audience testing.
_Avoid_: raw clip review, asset preflight

**Script Drift**:
Generated speech that does not match the approved script, including extra words,
missing words, substitutions, or provider-added lead-in phrases.
_Avoid_: creative variation, harmless ad lib

**Asset Repair**:
A local edit that fixes a generated audio asset without changing the approved
script, such as trimming provider-added words from the start of a clip.
_Avoid_: regeneration, rewrite

**Technical Preflight**:
The non-taste part of an Asset Quality Gate, checking measurable audio and
metadata facts before creative review.
_Avoid_: creative review, audience test

**Preflight Transcription**:
A transcript created from generated audio solely to compare the spoken words
against the approved script during Technical Preflight.
_Avoid_: final transcript, subtitles

**Creative Critic**:
The taste and message part of an Asset Quality Gate, judging whether an asset or
rendered advert feels credible, clear, and worth continuing.
_Avoid_: technical check, paid test

**External Test Candidate**:
A rendered advert that has survived local quality gates and is ready to be shown
to paid or outside reviewers.
_Avoid_: raw asset, unfinished edit

**Reusable Asset**:
An audio asset that has enough quality, metadata, and searchability to be used
again in future adverts or videos.
_Avoid_: throwaway take

**Rejected Asset**:
An audio asset or rendered advert kept for evidence and search, but marked as not
for reuse unless deliberately revived.
_Avoid_: deleted asset
