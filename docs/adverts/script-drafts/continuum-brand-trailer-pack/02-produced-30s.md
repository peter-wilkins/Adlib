## Script

You know the feeling.

You already thought about this. You solved part of it. You made a decision.

But now it is buried in a chat, a note, a repo, a tab, or your own exhausted
memory.

Continuum is built around continuity of thought: spoken captures, append-only
source history, and context that can be restored when the thread matters again.

Not another dashboard. Not another chatbot.

A memory surface for work that keeps coming back.

Continuum. Preserve the thread.

Go to continuumkit.org.
