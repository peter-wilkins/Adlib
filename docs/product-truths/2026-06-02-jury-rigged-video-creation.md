# Product Truth: Jury-Rigged Video Creation Software

Date: 2026-06-02

## Elevator Pitch

Jury-Rigged is an early concept for making playful physical challenge videos: design the puzzle, hide useful materials in plain sight, give teams earned advantages, capture the chaos, and turn the result into a watchable story.

## Quick Start Guide

1. Pick one physical challenge, such as a final race or build task.
2. Define teams, safety limits, available materials, and hidden useful parts.
3. Add earlier mini-challenges that can earn head starts or better materials.
4. Use the tool to generate a run sheet, shot list, scoring rules, and edit beats.
5. Capture the event and mark good moments.
6. Generate an edit plan, title options, narration prompts, and publish checklist.

## One Sentence

Jury-Rigged is a concept for designing and capturing physical challenge videos where teams discover useful parts hidden inside the game.

## User And Job

- User: Peter or a small creator/team designing playful engineering competitions.
- Job: turn a loose challenge idea into something safe, filmable, scored, and editable.

## Truth Levels

### Proven

- The concept appears in the 2026-06-02 Keep import:
  - Crystal Maze-style earlier puzzles.
  - Winning teams get an advantage in the final race.
  - Useful parts are hidden in plain sight.
  - Teams may choose paddling, swimming, a motor, a lathe, or a sail.
- The Good Moment Marker notes already define a media-agnostic capture primitive for saving useful moments.

### Working But Unproven

- No dedicated app exists yet.
- The exact product may be a video-production tool, an event design tool, or a game-show format planner.
- It needs safety constraints before any real physical challenge.

### Aspirational

- Generate a challenge bible, run sheet, shot list, score rules, editing structure, and narration.
- Use video/audio/text good-moment markers to preserve the best parts of the event.
- Turn small team chaos into a coherent YouTube-ready story.

### False Or Stale

- It is not working editing software yet.
- It is not a replacement for risk assessment, first aid, water safety, or supervision.
- It should not become a giant production suite before the first challenge is proven.

### Private Only

- Raw footage, participant details, location details, and safety notes stay private until reviewed.

## Smallest Honest Pitch

Jury-Rigged is a concept for a creator tool that helps design, capture, and edit playful physical challenges. It starts with run sheets and story structure, not automatic video magic.
