## Script

Before anyone draws a plan, the land has already started speaking.

Where does the water rush away? Where does it pause? Where is the soil bare,
wet, cracked, or quietly recovering?

Checkdam visit videos are field notes from water-cycle restoration work:
watching, asking, measuring, and learning before we claim answers.

Not final design advice. Not miracle language.

Just practical attention to water, land, and what might help them heal.

Checkdam. Restore the water. Restore the land.

Go to checkdam.org.
