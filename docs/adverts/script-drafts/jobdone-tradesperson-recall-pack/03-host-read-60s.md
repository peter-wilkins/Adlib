# JobDone Recall Pack - Host Read 60s

Project: JobDone  
Format: host-read talking points, around 60 seconds  
Audience: tradespeople, small contractors, field-service operators  
Goal: explain why confirmed recall matters  
Claim posture: current truth / MVP direction  
Source: `docs/product-truths/2026-06-02-jobdone.md`

## Mandatory Copy Points

- JobDone is phone-first and mobile-first.
- Capture can be voice note, text note, photo, link, or shared contact.
- Review and confirmation matter before an Entry becomes part of the timeline.
- Recall is grounded in source Entries.
- Do not claim full accounting, CRM replacement, or always-correct AI.

## Read

Most tradespeople do not have a memory problem. They have an admin timing
problem.

When you are leaving the job, you know exactly what happened. But you are also
loading the van, answering the phone, getting to the next place, and trying not
to spend your evening typing notes into a system you hate.

JobDone is built around that moment.

You capture what happened on the phone: voice note, text note, photo, link, or
shared contact. Then you review it, confirm the contact, location, and tags, and
the confirmed Entry becomes part of your timeline.

Later, when someone asks, "What did you do at Mrs Jones last time?", Recall is
designed to work from those source Entries instead of making up a clever answer.

It is not a full CRM. It is not finished accounting. It is job memory with a
confirmation step.

JobDone. Remember the job.
