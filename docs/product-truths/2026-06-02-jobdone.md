# Product Truth: JobDone

Date: 2026-06-02

## Elevator Pitch

JobDone is a mobile-first voice log for self-employed tradespeople: speak what happened on a job, confirm it once, and later recall the right job, contact, location, material, or follow-up without searching your memory.

## Quick Start Guide

1. Capture a voice note, text note, photo, link, or shared contact on the phone.
2. Review the transcript and the predicted location and contact.
3. Confirm it when it is true; the confirmed Entry becomes part of the immutable Timeline.
4. Ask Recall questions such as "What did I do at Mrs Jones last time?"
5. Use selected Entries later as proof, share packs, or approval evidence.

## One Sentence

JobDone turns messy job notes into confirmed operational memory for tradespeople.

## User And Job

- User: self-employed tradespeople and small operators who lose important details in voice notes, messages, memory, or repeated site visits.
- Job: capture what happened now, then retrieve the right evidence later without building a full admin habit.

## Truth Levels

### Proven

- The JobDone repo exists at `/home/peter/jobdone`.
- The README defines the product as a mobile-first, local-first voice log for self-employed tradespeople.
- The domain language in `CONTEXT.md` is already unusually mature: Capture, Entry, Timeline, Recall, Location, Contact, Share Pack, Approval Request, and Team are named.
- MVP rules preserve local-first behaviour and warn against silent data loss.
- Recall property-testing docs define the proof direction: retrieval grounding, expected source IDs, excluded sources, and deterministic oracle checks.

### Working But Unproven

- The exact first public advert has not been written.
- The MVP is not yet proven with real tradesperson users.
- Recall proof is being built; do not advertise it as finished property-tested retrieval until tests pass.
- Multi-team, approval, reward, and family/work variants are domain-shaped but not all product-finished.

### Aspirational

- A tradesperson can use JobDone all day from the phone with minimal admin.
- Recall becomes boringly trustworthy: right source Entries, wrong Entries excluded, uncertainty shown when needed.
- Share Packs and Approval Requests turn confirmed Entries into practical proof.

### False Or Stale

- JobDone is not a generic chatbot.
- JobDone is not a full accounting or office admin package.
- JobDone is not allowed to silently discard unconfirmed captures.
- "Customer" is not the current preferred language; use Contact and Location.

### Private Only

- Raw user data, test captures, and personal development logs stay private unless explicitly approved and redacted.

## Smallest Honest Pitch

JobDone is a phone-first memory system for tradespeople. Capture what happened, confirm it into a timeline, and recall the right job details later from grounded source Entries instead of relying on memory or scattered messages.
