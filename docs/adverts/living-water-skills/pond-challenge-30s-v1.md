# Living Water Skills: Pond Challenge 30s Advert V1

Date: 2026-06-03

Source truth: `docs/product-truths/2026-06-02-living-water-skills.md`

## Intent

Make Living Water Skills feel like a friendly outdoor mentor for a young learner:
the learner captures evidence, asks what to do next, gets safe guidance, and is
encouraged to submit the next challenge for review.

## Voice Roles

- **Young learner**: original Maya audition voice; excited, proud, outdoors, not stage-school polished.
- **App mentor**: River, processed through the phone-speaker treatment; human-warm mentor performance, calm, kind, practical.
- **Narrator**: Lily voice direction; 20s/cool/hopeful energy, lightly aspirational, not charity-ad sombre.

## Sound Bed

- Garden birds and light pond water.
- Phone-recording feel for the learner and app interaction.
- Keep it fresh and outside; no dramatic music needed for V1.

## Script

```text
SFX: birds, a little water movement, phone held outside near a pond.

Young learner:
It’s working! My pond’s holding water now. What can I do to attract nature?
Especially frogs.

App mentor:
Nice work. Add native pond plants, make a shallow edge, and leave some messy
shelter nearby. Don’t collect frogs or frogspawn from the wild. If the habitat
is right, the right creatures will find it.

Young learner:
So basically make it a good home and be patient?

App mentor:
Exactly. I think you’re ready to submit your Level 2 Pond Challenge now.

Narrator:
Living Water Skills. Learn outside. Capture the evidence. Earn your levels.
Grow from curious to capable.
```

## Product Truth QA

- Uses the spine: learn, observe, capture, review, story.
- Says the learner is ready to submit a challenge, not automatically certified.
- Gives safety-aware ecological advice: do not collect frogs or frogspawn from the wild.
- Avoids claiming guaranteed ecological outcomes.
- Keeps the app aspirational but aligned with the planned logbook / review ladder.
- Uses `earn your levels` rather than `get qualified` until formal accreditation is real.

## Resolved V1 Choices

1. Learner voice: original Maya audition voice, using the V1 audition settings rather than the younger V2 prompt.
2. App mentor voice: River, rendered with phone-speaker tinniness in the mix.
3. Narrator: Lily.
4. Final line for V1: `Learn outside. Capture the evidence. Earn your levels. Grow from curious to capable.`
5. Deferred bolder line: `get qualified`, once the product has real accreditation or a clear qualification pathway behind it.
