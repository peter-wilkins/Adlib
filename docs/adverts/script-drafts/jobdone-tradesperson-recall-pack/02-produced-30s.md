## Script

At the end of a job, you do remember the important bit.

The stiff valve. The spare part. The awkward access. The dog called Charlie.

The problem is remembering it two months later.

JobDone is a phone-first voice log for tradespeople. Speak what happened, review
the transcript, confirm the contact and location, and keep it in a timeline you
can ask later.

Built for job memory, not office admin.

Just confirmed job memory when the callback comes.

JobDone. Remember the job.

Part of the Continuum family.

Go to jobdone.continuumkit.org.
